Roots of Q Polynomials Between specified intervals

There’s not just the cauliflower. In fact, the cauliflower appears to be the shared space between several unique fractals.

Between 0.1 and 0.2 we see the horizontal outline of the cauliflower, plus a trace of the “eye” that once haunted our plots. ![[ArtOfRoots_Between01and02.png]]

0.2 and 0.3 give the most spectacular image. There are at least 3 fractals at play: one with the outline of a cello that seems composed of all variety of roots; one clove-like, girded by purple, which demonstrates six-fold symmetry…

![[ArtOfRoots_Between02and03.png]]

Here again the eye of Q gazes upon the world through a slit pupil.

![[ArtOfRoots_Between03and04.png]]

We see something like a displaced cauliflower along the outside, with an irregular dog bone within. ![[ArtOfRoots_Between04and05.png]]
